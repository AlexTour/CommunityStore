Improving Logistics and Shortening Value Chains

Purpose and Scope

The RUE addresses challenges related to long value chains with many intermediaries between producers and consumers, fragmented distribution, and limited visibility across rural–urban value chains.

  • Application at regional level
  • Use in planning activities

What the RUE Provides

  • Digital learning platform addressing lack of experience in food production
  • Tool that shortens the journey between producer and consumer

Intended Users

  • Farmers
  • Cooperatives
  • Food producers
  • University students
  • Policy-makers in agriculture
